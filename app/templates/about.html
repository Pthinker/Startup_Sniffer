Bio: Ph.D Candidate, Department of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Notre Dame. My research focuses on data mining and bioinformatics.

StartupSniffer is incubated from InsightDataScience in 2013. The first tool is a predictive model using Random Forest classification to predict startup outcome (IPO/acquired) using crunchbase.com data. The second tool is a leaderboard for more than 1,000 early stage startups. Ranking is based on twitter, bitly.com and Angellist data.

Interested in the source codes or want to contribute? Check it out on Github!
